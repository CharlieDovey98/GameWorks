Play Space Invaders

Tutorial :

To play Space Invaders you will need to use the arrow keys; left and right to move your space ship across the horizon.
The up arrow key or the space bar will allow your ship to shoot towards the alien invasion.

Objective :

Your objective is to soot down the decending rows of alien space ships while dodging their retaliatory fire.
The aliens will move side to ide and shoot progressively faster as you progress through the rounds making each round harder and harder.
The aliens will increase their number and their health further into the game calling for backup from their larger alien spacecraft.
The last level you will face will be a boss level with their leader present.
This will be a formidible oponent with buffed abilities including fire rate and health.
Good luck on your journey should you choose to accept pilot.
