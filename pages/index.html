Play Space Invaders

Tutorial :

To play Space Invaders you will need to use the arrow keys; left and right to move your space ship across the horizon.
The up arrow key or the space bar will allow your ship to shoot towards the alien invasion.

Objective :

Your objective is to shoot down the descending rows of alien spaceships while dodging their retaliatory fire.
The aliens will move side to side and shoot faster as you progress through the rounds making each round harder and harder.
The aliens will increase in number the further you progress the game as they call for backup from their larger alien spacecraft.
The last level will be a boss level with their leader present.
This will be a formidable opponent with buffed abilities including fire rate and health.
Good luck on your journey should you choose to accept pilot.
