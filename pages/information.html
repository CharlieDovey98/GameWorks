Game Works

Space Invaders is a classic arcade game that was first released in 1978 by Taito, a Japanese video game developer. It is one of the most iconic and influential video games in the history of the medium. Developed by Tomohiro Nishikado, "Space Invaders" was among the earliest shooting games and played a significant role in shaping the gaming industry.

Have a look at the Space invaders Wikipedia page to learn the vast history of the game or to explore the world of Arcade games.
